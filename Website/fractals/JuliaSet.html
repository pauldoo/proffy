Drag the orange dot around to see the Julia Set corresponding to each point on the Mandelbrot Set. The sketchy green outline is a rough approximation of the shape of the Julia Set.

Click the button at the bottom of the applet to open a new window showing a fully interactive detailed view of your current Julia Set.

Pan: Click and drag.

Zoom: Mousewheel or +/-.

Rotate: Click and drag whilst holding shift.
